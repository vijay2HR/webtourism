ERNAKULAM DISTRICT

District in Kerala DescriptionErnakulam is a district of Kerala, India. Situated in the central part of that state. Spanning an area of about 2,407 km², Ernakulam district is home to over 12% of Kerala’s population. Its headquarters is located at Kakkanad. Ernakulam district includes Kochi city, which is known as the commercial capital of Kerala. Wikipedia Area: 2,407 km² Population: 32.8 lakhs (2011) ISO 3166 code: Kerala
