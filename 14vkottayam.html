KOTTAYAM DISTRICT

The Kottayam District is one of fourteen districts in the state of Kerala, India. Kottayam comprises six municipal towns: Kottayam, Changanassery, Pala, Erattupetta, Ettumanoor, and Vaikom. ... The district is bordered by hills in the east, and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west. State: Kerala Country: India Region: Central Travancore
